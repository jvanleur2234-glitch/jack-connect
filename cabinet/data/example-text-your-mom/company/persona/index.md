# Customer Persona

## Core User

The user is a warm, busy, slightly overwhelmed adult who genuinely cares about friends and family but drops replies, forgets to check in, and feels low-grade guilt about it.

## Emotional Truths

- They do not need another productivity lecture.
- They want help following through without feeling like a bad person.
- They laugh at their own avoidance when the copy is kind and honest.

## Product Promise

Text Your Mom helps you keep tiny relationship promises before they turn into "wow, I have not replied in eight days."

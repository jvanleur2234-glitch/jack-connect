# Company Strategy

Text Your Mom exists to reduce tiny relationship failures before they become emotional distance.

## Strategy

1. Win with a painfully relatable problem everyone recognizes in one sentence.
2. Use humor-first content to earn attention on social channels.
3. Convert attention into a daily-use reminder product with strong emotional payoff.
4. Retain users by making the app feel caring, not nagging.

## Strategic Bets

- Humor-led short-form growth beats polished wellness branding.
- Reddit is a better research and empathy channel than a vanity channel.
- Product quality matters because reminders are intimate; one annoying bug can destroy trust.

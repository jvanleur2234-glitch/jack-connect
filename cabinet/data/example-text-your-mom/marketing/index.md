# Marketing

The marketing branch holds two imported child cabinets with very different jobs:

- `tiktok` turns relatable guilt and humor into attention and installs
- `reddit` turns real conversations into insight, messaging, and community-led growth

Both cabinets inherit the company strategy and customer persona from the parent cabinet, but each one stays focused on its own channel behavior.

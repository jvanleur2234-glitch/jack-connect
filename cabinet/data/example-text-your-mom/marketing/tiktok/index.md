# TikTok Growth

This imported child cabinet turns the emotional tension behind Text Your Mom into funny, swipeable, high-volume short-form creative.

## Positioning

The tone is not "be a better daughter." The tone is "you absolutely meant to reply three days ago and now you are pretending the notification does not exist."

## Team

- Trend Scout
- Script Writer
- Image Creator
- Post Optimizer

## Core Assets

- `content-ideas.csv` for the backlog
- `competitors.csv` for adjacent apps and content styles
- `briefs/` for weekly campaign framing
- `analytics/` for performance notes
- `content-calendar/` for a quick visual view of the week

This cabinet is installed but paused by default so it behaves like a newly imported module.

# Brief: "Text your mom before she sends ?"

**Format:** 5-slide carousel  
**Hook:** "The most dangerous text on earth is just a question mark"  
**Status:** Script Ready  
**Visual tone:** High contrast, chat-bubble heavy, punchline delivered via silence

---

## Concept

A single `?` from mom is not a question. It is a verdict.  
This carousel lives inside that specific dread — the moment you realize the messages have stopped coming and something worse has replaced them.  
No yelling, no guilt trip. Just one character. That's how you know it's bad.

---

## Slide Breakdown

### Slide 1 — The Setup
**Visual:** Phone lock screen. One notification. `Mom: ?`  
**Text overlay:** "The most dangerous text on earth is just a question mark"  
**Tone:** Ominous. No color except the notification red.

### Slide 2 — The Evidence
**Visual:** Chat thread zoomed out. Last message from user: `haha` — with two blue read receipts.  
**Text overlay:** "She sent a paragraph. You said 'haha'. She saw you read it."  
**Tone:** The crime is visible. No music swell needed.

### Slide 3 — The Escalation Pattern
**Visual:** Three chat bubbles in a row. `"Hey! Hope you're good 😊"` → `"??"` → `"?"`  
**Text overlay:** "This is not a question. This is a communication downgrade."  
**Tone:** Dry. Let the bubbles do the work.

### Slide 4 — The Translation
**Visual:** Single `?` bubble. Large. Centered.  
**Text overlay:** "Translated: I raised you. I can see your read receipts. Respond."  
**Tone:** Deadpan. No exclamation marks.

### Slide 5 — The CTA
**Visual:** App reminder notification — casual, not pushy. "Hey, your mom texted 2 days ago 💬"  
**Text overlay:** "Text her before it becomes a question mark."  
**Tone:** Warm landing. The joke resolved.

---

## Creative Notes

- Keep all slide backgrounds dark or desaturated — the notification badge red is the only accent color
- Chat bubbles should look real, not illustrated
- No stock photos of families smiling
- The joke peaks at slide 4 — do not explain it further
- Sound: silence or a single notification ping SFX works better than trending audio here

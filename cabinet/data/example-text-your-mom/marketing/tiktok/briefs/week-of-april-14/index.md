# Brief — Week of April 14

First activation week. Two scripts ready to shoot. Both pull from the "reply guilt" emotional core without pushing the product. Humor first, product second.

---

## Script 1 — "You Liked the Photo But Did Not Answer the Text"

**Format:** Talking head, 30–45 seconds
**Priority:** High (was "Script Ready" in backlog)
**Emotional angle:** Shared guilt, zero judgment
**Product mention:** Soft close only

**Hook (on-screen text + spoken):**
> You liked the photo but did not answer the text.

**Full script:**

---

Let me tell you what you did.

She posted something. You liked it. You put your phone down. You told yourself that was basically a reply.

It was not basically a reply.

She knows it was not basically a reply. You both know. Nobody's saying anything.

[pause — hold camera, let it land]

A like is you saying "I saw this."
A text is you saying "I see you."

These are different things.

[lighter, almost to self]

Anyway. I made an app for this because I did it for six months and it made me feel terrible. Link in bio. Go text your person.

---

**Notes:**
- No teleprompter energy. This works best if it feels like a conversation you interrupted.
- The pause before "She knows" is load-bearing. Don't rush it.
- End should feel like an exhale, not a pitch.
- Caption: *a like is not a text. a like is not a text. a like is not a text.*

---

## Script 2 — "The Most Dangerous Text on Earth Is a Question Mark"

**Format:** 5-slide carousel (text on static or light motion)
**Priority:** Critical (highest-rated idea in backlog)
**Emotional angle:** Specific dread everyone recognizes
**Product mention:** Final slide only

**Slides:**

---

**Slide 1:**
The most dangerous text on earth is one character long.

**Slide 2:**
It comes from your mom.
It reads: ?

**Slide 3:**
It means:
I texted you four days ago.
You have been active on Instagram.
I am not mad.
I am just asking.

**Slide 4:**
She is a little mad.

**Slide 5:**
Text Your Mom.
Because the question mark is coming either way.
[logo + link]

---

**Notes:**
- Keep Slide 3 pacing slow if animated — each line should land separately.
- Slide 4 is the punchline. One line. Don't add anything.
- Design: plain text, high contrast, no decorative elements. The joke is the visual.
- Caption options:
  - *she is a little mad*
  - *? = I raised you better than this*
  - *one character. devastating.*

# TikTok Analytics

## What To Watch

- Hook retention in the first second
- Saves and shares on highly relatable formats
- Comments that mention "this is literally me"
- Install lift from content that includes a soft product reveal

## Early Hypothesis

The best-performing posts will not be the most polished ones. They will be the ones that feel like a confession.

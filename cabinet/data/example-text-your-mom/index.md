# Text Your Mom

Text Your Mom is a playful consumer app that helps people stay close to family and friends by nudging them to reply, check in, and keep tiny relationship promises before avoidance turns into guilt.

## Why This Example Works

- Everybody knows the feeling of meaning to reply "later" and realizing it has been five days.
- The product is emotionally real, funny, and easy to explain.
- It gives the company a natural growth loop across TikTok, Reddit, and app product work.

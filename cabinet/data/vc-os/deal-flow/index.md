## Sourcing Philosophy

Warp sources deals through 5 channels:

| Channel | % of Pipeline | Quality |
|---|---|---|
| Partner network + warm intros | 40% | Highest |
| YC / accelerator alumni | 20% | High |
| Fellowship program | 15% | High |
| Inbound (web form, cold email) | 15% | Variable |
| Conference + events | 10% | Variable |

**Rule of thumb:** The best deals come through the partner network. Inbound with no mutual connection gets 48-hour response, not same-day.

---

## Decision Framework

At each stage, the question changes:

- **Sourcing → First Look:** Is this worth 2 hours of a partner's time?
- **First Look → Partner Mtg:** Does this fit our thesis AND is the founder someone we'd work with for 10 years?
- **Partner Mtg → DD:** Do we believe this is a fund-returning outcome if it works?
- **DD → Term Sheet:** Are the risks we found acceptable, and are we the right investor for this company?
- **Term Sheet → Close:** Can we negotiate terms that are fair to all parties?

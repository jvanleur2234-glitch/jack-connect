## Batch 3 Target Profile

Warp is specifically looking for Batch 3 companies in:
- AI-native enterprise tools (replacing legacy SaaS point solutions)
- Climate tech with revenue potential in 18 months
- Developer tooling with strong OSS → paid conversion stories
- B2B fintech with clear regulatory moat

*Not looking for:* consumer apps, crypto/web3, pure research, hardware-only.

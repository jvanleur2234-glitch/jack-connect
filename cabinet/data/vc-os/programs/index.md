# Warp Ventures Programs

Two structured programs to surface early-stage founders and build the Warp community.

---

## Warp Fellowship

A 12-week paid research and exploration program for exceptional early-stage builders who haven't yet started a company. Fellows get office space, a $5K/month stipend, and access to the full Warp partner network.

**Goal:** Convert 20% of fellows into future portfolio founders. The Stackwise deal came from a Fellowship alum.

- **Cohort 2:** Currently running. 8 fellows. Demo Day: May 14, 2026
- **Cohort 3:** Applications open June 2026. Target: 10 fellows. Start: September 2026

See [[fellowship-2026]] for Cohort 2 details.

---

## Warp Accelerator

A 10-week intensive program for pre-seed startups. $150K investment for 6% equity. 8 companies per batch. Culminates in a Demo Day with 50+ invited investors.

**Goal:** Deploy $1.2M per batch, generate 8 seed-stage companies with Warp as the first institutional investor.

- **Batch 3:** Recruiting now. Target start: July 2026
- **Batch 2:** Graduated October 2025. 2 of 8 companies have raised Seed rounds.

See [[accelerator]] for Batch 3 details and alumni.

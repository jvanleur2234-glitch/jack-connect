## Standard LP Communication Rules

1. **Never share portfolio company financials** without explicit company consent and NDA
2. **Runway concerns** — describe directionally (e.g., "18-month runway, initiating raise") without exact numbers
3. **TVPI/DPI** — only share audited figures or clearly mark as "unaudited estimate"
4. **Personnel** — never discuss internal team conflicts in LP communications
5. **All letters** reviewed by legal counsel before distribution

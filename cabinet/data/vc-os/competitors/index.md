- Bio/health push means less bandwidth for pure B2B SaaS at Series A. Opportunity for Warp.

### Sequoia
- Sequoia passed on Synthica (Warp's First Look pipeline) — unclear why. Jordan following up.

### First Round

### Accel
- **NEW (Apr 13):** Led Undo.io $4.5M seed (developer debugging). Active in DevTools at seed. Will likely compete for DevForge Series A. Sam should start warming Accel's DevTools partner now — better to be known before the raise than unknown at pitch time.

Runway Financial is MintLayer-adjacent but focused on startup finance vs. mid-market banking. Sam should pull competitive differentiation and make sure Elena's team has the comparison ready.

## Why MintLayer Wins

The mid-market CFO has 7–12 disconnected tools: ERP (NetSuite/SAP), treasury management, FP&A (Anaplan), payments (Stripe), FX (Bloomberg terminal), reporting (Excel). MintLayer replaces the glue code. The AI layer automates reconciliation, compliance flagging, and cash flow forecasting.

**Moat:** bank-grade security certifications (SOC2 Type II, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS Level 1) take 2+ years to acquire. New entrants can't skip this. MintLayer has all three.

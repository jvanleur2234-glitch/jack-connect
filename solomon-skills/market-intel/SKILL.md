# Market Intelligence Skill

Monitor Jack's farm area 24/7 and alert on opportunities.

## What to Watch

- **New listings** in farm area (same day they hit MLS)
- **Price reductions** on existing inventory
- **Expired listings** (owners who didn't sell — contact them)
- **New construction** permits pulled
- **Price shifts** in comparable neighborhoods
- **Days on market** trend changes

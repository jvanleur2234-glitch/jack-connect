# Client Nourisher Skill

Keep Jack's Sphere of Influence warm year-round with automated, personal touches.

## Touch Calendar

| Occasion | Timing | Message Type |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Birthday | 9 AM on the day | Personal text |
| Home anniversary (purchase date) | 9 AM on the day | "Congrats on X years in your home!" |
| Market update (quarterly) | First of quarter | Value-add newsletter |
| Just sold / bought | 1 week after close | "How's the new place?" |
| Holiday | Dec 1 | Holiday card |
| New listing in their neighborhood | Same day | "This just hit the market..." |

## Message Rules

- Never sound automated
- Reference something personal you know about them
- Offer value first, don't pitch
- Keep it under 3 sentences for texts

## CRM Integration

Log every touch in JackConnect CRM with:
- Date
- Type (birthday/anniversary/market_update/follow_up)
- Response received (if any)

## Weekly Summary

Every Friday: "This week you touched X clients. Y responded. Z have birthdays/anniversaries coming next week."

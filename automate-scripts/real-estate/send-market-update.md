## Steps

1. Open email client [key:ctrl+1]
2. Click "New Email" [click:coord=150,120]
3. Enter subject line [type:Your Monthly Market Update — [MONTH YYYY]]
4. Select template [click:coord=X,Y]
5. Personalize greeting [type:{CONTACT_NAME}]
6. Update market data fields [click:coord=X,Y] [type:{DATA}]
7. Add agent signature [key:ctrl+shift+s]
8. Send [key:ctrl+enter]

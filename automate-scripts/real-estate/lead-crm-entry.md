## Steps

1. Open CRM [click:coord=100,50] or [key:ctrl+2]
2. Click "Add New Lead" [click:coord=800,200]
3. Enter name [type:{LEAD_NAME}]
4. Enter phone [type:{LEAD_PHONE}]
5. Enter email [type:{LEAD_EMAIL}]
6. Select lead source [click:coord=X,Y]
7. Enter lead notes [type:{LEAD_NOTES}]
8. Set follow-up date [click:coord=X,Y]
9. Save lead [key:ctrl+s]
10. Confirm save [key:enter]

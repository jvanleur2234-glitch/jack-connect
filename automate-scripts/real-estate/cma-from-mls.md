## Steps

1. Open MLS browser [key:ctrl+l]
2. Navigate to MLS search [type:https://www.mls.com]
3. Search for subject property address [type:{ADDRESS}]
4. Pull up property details [click:coord=400,300]
5. Find comparable sales section [scroll_down]
6. Pull 3 most recent comps [click:coord=X,Y]
7. Screenshot each comp [key:ctrl+shift+s]
8. Open CMA template [key:ctrl+o] [type:CMA-Template.xlsx]
9. Fill in subject property details
10. Paste comp screenshots
11. Save as [ADDRESS]-CMA-YYYY-MM-DD [key:ctrl+s]
